Oatmeal with Nut Butter and Yogurt
Cal: 400
Protein: 30g
Sugar: 0g
Carbs: 50g
Creator: Jacob Lamp
Ingredients:
  1. Oats: 1 cup
  2. Milk: 1.5 cups
  3. Nut butter: 2 tbsp
  4. Yogurt: 1 cup
  5. Optional fruit or other toppings
Instructions:
  1. Mix oats and milk together in a microwaveable bowl. Microwave for 1.5 - 2 minutes, or let sit overnight. (Raw unsoaked oats are "safe to eat," but are worse for "digestibility and nutrient absorption," as said by healthline.com.)
  2. Add yogurt, nut butter, and other fruit or optional toppings. Serve with spoon.
Greek Ground Turkey Zucchini Meatballs
Cal: 560
Protein: 35g
Sugar: 4g
Carbs: 30g
Creator: Reddit User
Ingredients:
  1. 1 sm-med zucchini, shredded
  2. 1/4 yellow onion, shredded
  3. 1 lb ground turkey
  4. 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 tsp cumin
  7. 1/2 tsp cavenders greek seasoning
  8. 1/2 tsp blended chili flake
  9. 1/4 tsp black pepper
  10. 1/4 tsp sumac
  11. 1/4 cup chopped cilantro or mint or parsley (or all)
  12. 1 egg
Instructions:
  1. Mix all together and sear in neutral oil.
  2. Served with charred tomatoes, chopped spinach, crumbled feta, and creamy cucumbers: 1/2 cucumber plus 2 tsp sour cream or yogurt, 2 tsp lemon juice, garlic, sprinkle sumac, salt and pepper.
  3. Served with lemony roasted potatoes.
Homemade yogurt
Cal: 143
Protein: 12g
Sugar: 16g
Carbs: 16g
Creator: Reddit User
Ingredients:
  1. Milk
  2. Yogurt with active culture
  3. Optional sugar or fruit
Instructions:
  1. Boil milk
  2. Let milk cool (If you want to be scientific 90F-105F, but I don't think it matters if it goes below 90F. It should be bath water temp)
  3. Add yogurt with active culture(the cheapest 4 pack of walmart great value brand yogurt has it, look under the ingredients)
  4. Let sit for 6-24 hours(other recipes say to put it in a warm place or a place that wont cool it down immediately, I don't think it matters) and put it in your refrigerator when done.
  5. I add 1 tbsp sugar per cup of yogurt. You can add vanilla or fruit, I don't bother.
  6. I like 9 hours of sitting time, it reminds me of Yoplait, liquidy and not sour. If you want it firmer and more sour, you can wait longer like 24 hours.
  7. You can use your new yogurt as the starter for next time.
Wonderful Omelette
Cal: 410
Protein: 30g
Sugar: 0g
Carbs: 10g
Creator: Jacob Lamp
Ingredients:
  1. Eggs: 2
  2. Butter
  3. Salt: 1 tsp
  4. Pepper: 1 tsp
  5. Nuts: 2 tbsp
  6. Chives: 1 tbsp
  7. Cheese: 1/4 cup
  8. Hot sauce or other sauce
  9. Chunks of ham or other meat: 1/4 cup
Instructions:
  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt, pepper, half of the cheese, half of the chives, and half of the nuts. Whisk a desired amount.
  2. Place a pan on the stove, turn the heat on and spread butter on the pan.
  3. Pour the egg mixture on the pan, let it cook until it is strong.
  4. Add the rest of the toppings onto one side of the omelette, fold the other side of the omelette over, place on plate and serve with the sauce on the side.
